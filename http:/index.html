About Us
Ameri-Mark, Inc. is a premium quality real estate appraisal firm with more than 25 years of experience. 
    

    
Our good old-fashioned work ethic and thorough research provides our clients with rock-solid appraisal results that protect lenders collateralizing loans. We excel in this area of the appraisal business because we carefully dissect the appraisal problem.

    
In addition to the typical appraisal procedures, we attempt to provide a list of deficient items requiring repairs to prepare the subject property for an arm's length market value sales transaction. The cost to cure these deficient items is then estimated and stated in the report.

A huge amount of closed sales data is assembled and an analysis is performed to estimate the ranges of properties similar to the subject in a number of range brackets. Additional overall steps are taken to properly bracket the subject into the current available market data.

A comparison of recent closed sales and current listings is analyzed to determine possible changes in the market climate. Additional photos are provided, if necessary, to verify and confirm the condition of the subject to the client.

We work with owners and realtors to establish a listing price. You may view narrated digital video virtual tours illustrating "items of disrepair" and "deferred maintenance items." You may choose some or all of the above items, but regardless, we are looking forward to building a solid long-term relationship with you.
